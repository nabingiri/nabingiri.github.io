Nabin Giri

Postdoc at Berkeley Lab

Berkeley, CA

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at Berkeley Lab, where I apply AI to structural biology. I did my PhD in Computer Science (minor in Statistics) at the University of Missouri under Dr. Jianlin Cheng and received the Outstanding PhD Student Award. During my PhD, I interned at BIOVIA and Amazon Science. View my dissertation slides here.

Prior to my PhD, I completed an MSc in Computer Science at the University of Central Missouri under Dr. Yui Man Lui. My thesis, Recommendation System Using Factorization Model and MapReduce Framework, received the Graduate Research Award. View my thesis here.

Before pursuing my master's degree, I worked as a Software Engineer at Capgemini and led a team as a System Engineer at Max International.

Research

I'm broadly interested in AI for science, with my current work focused on developing computational methods for structural biology.
